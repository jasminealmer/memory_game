Welcome to The Schmemory Game!

How do I play?

1. Click on two cards of your choice.

2. If the cards are a match, you continue to play two more cards.

3. If the cards are no match, the cards will flip back and you can start over with two new cards.

4. When all cards are matched - you win!

Click again and LET'S GO!
